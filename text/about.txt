Three crazy queer-dos and their straight best friend navigate post-grad life in New York City. Wyatt, Jesse, Chuck and Scarlett soon realize that expensive college degrees guarantee them nothing but their monthly student loan bills. The real world is driving them crazy, but their friendships with each other are keeping them stable, mostly.

Hard Feelings is going to serve you heartbreak, friendship, love, drag, and truthful insight on real issues queer millennials face on a daily basis; because despite what mainstream television and Hollywood tells you, there is more to being a queer person than stereotypes and coming out of the closet.
